Welcome Home

Where faith grows, hope flourishes, and love transforms lives

Welcome to Springfield Faith Assembly!

Thank you for taking a few minutes to get to know us. No matter who you are or where you have been, you are welcome here.

Life can be messy, and we are all imperfect people, but we are pursuing a transforming relationship with God. We believe in community, being intentional about our growth, and serving those around us. We are excited about what God is doing in our church, and we would like for you to get connected with us as we move forward.

Our Mission

To Love God, Grow together, and Share hope.

Our Core Values

Spirit empowered - Through worship, preaching, altar, prayer, and emerging focus.

Friendly - In encounters and atmosphere.

Community - Inside and outside the church through connect groups, Bible study, and community involvement by building lasting relationships.

Equipping and Engaging - Life in groups and ministry in teams.

Outreach - Think global; act local.
